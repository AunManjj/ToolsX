How to Use Your Chosen Logo & Favicon

  1. Choose Your Logo

    Select one of the four logo options above that best matches your brand identity.

    // Add to your HTML head:
    <link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="logo.svg">
  2. Choose Your Favicon

    Pick a favicon that works best at small sizes and for user recognition.

    // Multiple favicon sizes:
    <link rel="icon" sizes="16x16" href="favicon-16.png">
    <link rel="icon" sizes="32x32" href="favicon-32.png">
  3. Save as SVG or PNG

    Export your chosen design. SVGs are recommended for logos (scalable), PNGs for favicons (better browser support).

    // For best quality:
    SVG: Logo files (infinite scalability)
    PNG: Favicon at 16x16, 32x32, 64x64, 128x128
  4. Update Your Website

    Replace the existing favicon in your HTML and update any logo references in your site.

    <link rel="apple-touch-icon" href="apple-touch-icon.png">
    <link rel="manifest" href="site.webmanifest">
  5. Test Across Browsers

    Clear your browser cache and verify the favicon appears correctly in browser tabs, bookmarks, and address bars.
